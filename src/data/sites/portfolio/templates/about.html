About the Studio

Studio Meridian was founded in 2018 with a simple belief: great design is the intersection of clarity, craft, and empathy. We're a tight-knit team of strategists, designers, and engineers who partner with brands at pivotal moments — launches, pivots, and scale.

Our work spans identity systems, digital products, and immersive web experiences. We approach every project with the same rigor, whether it's a two-week sprint or a year-long engagement. The result is work that's thoughtful, lasting, and unmistakably intentional.
