Our New Summer Cold Brew

Every summer we rethink our cold drinks menu, and this year we went deeper than ever. Our head roaster James spent three months dialing in a new cold brew blend — a washed Colombian lot from Huila with notes of dark cherry, chocolate, and brown sugar. It's smooth enough to drink straight and complex enough to stand up to milk.

We're offering it three ways this summer. The classic black cold brew is steeped for 18 hours and served over ice — simple and clean. The vanilla oat milk version adds house-made vanilla syrup and Oatly Barista for something creamy without being heavy. And then there's the espresso tonic: a double shot pulled over tonic water and ice, finished with a lemon twist. It's bright, fizzy, and surprisingly refreshing on a hot afternoon.

All three are available starting today, and we'll be running them through September. If you're curious about the beans, James does a tasting every Saturday morning at 9am — swing by the bar and he'll walk you through the lot. We also have a limited batch of the same Colombian in retail bags if you want to brew at home.

Written by James Chen, Head Roaster
