Our Mission

Making deployment invisible so you can focus on what matters.

Launchpad was founded in 2024 by a group of engineers who were tired of spending more time managing infrastructure than building products. We had been through the cycle too many times: configure the CI pipeline, wrestle with container orchestration, debug mysterious network issues at 2am.
We believe deployment should be invisible. When you push code, it should just work. No YAML files, no build scripts, no waiting. Your team should spend its energy on the product your customers actually use, not on the plumbing that holds it together.
That belief drives everything we build. Every feature we ship is measured against one question: does this let developers focus more on their code and less on everything else?

What We Believe

Simplicity wins
The best infrastructure is the kind you never think about. We hide complexity so you don't have to manage it.
Speed matters
Fast deploys, fast pages, fast support. We optimize for velocity at every layer of the stack.
Trust by default
Open pricing, public status page, transparent incident reports. We earn trust by being honest.

Our Team

We're a team of 24 based in San Francisco, with roots at AWS, Vercel, and Stripe. We're backed by Sequoia Capital and hiring across engineering, design, and developer relations.
