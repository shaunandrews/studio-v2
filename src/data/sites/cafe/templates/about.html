Our Story

Downstreet Café started in 2018 as a tiny espresso counter on Oak Street in Brooklyn. What began as a passion project between two friends — a roaster and a baker — quickly became a neighborhood fixture. We believed that a great cup of coffee could anchor a community, and seven years later, that idea still drives everything we do.

We source single-origin beans from small farms in Colombia, Ethiopia, and Guatemala, roasting them in-house every week. Our kitchen runs on the same philosophy: seasonal ingredients, honest preparation, nothing unnecessary. The menu changes with what's fresh — stone fruit in summer, root vegetables in winter, and Rosa's pastries all year round.

“Great coffee, honest food, and a place to slow down.”
