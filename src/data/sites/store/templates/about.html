Our Story

Thread & Co. started in a Portland garage in 2018 with a simple idea: make everyday products that last a lifetime, sell them directly to the people who use them, and cut out everything in between.

We were tired of disposable goods and inflated prices. So we partnered with small workshops around the world — watchmakers in Japan, leather workers in Italy, textile mills in Portugal — to create products built with real craft and honest materials.

By selling direct-to-consumer, we keep prices fair and the relationship personal. No middlemen, no markups, no mystery. Just well-made things from people who care.

Lifetime Guarantee

Every product we sell comes with a lifetime guarantee. If it breaks, wears out, or doesn't hold up the way you expected, we'll repair or replace it — no questions asked. We believe the best way to reduce waste is to make things that don't need replacing.

Our Values

Transparency
We share our costs, our margins, and the story behind every product.
Durability
Every item is built to last. Quality materials, expert craftsmanship.
Sustainability
Less waste, fewer replacements, and responsible sourcing across the board.
