About

I'm Jordan Atwell, a landscape and wildlife photographer based in the Pacific Northwest. For over a decade I've been chasing light through old-growth forests, alpine meadows, and remote coastline.

My work focuses on the quiet, fleeting moments in nature — the way fog settles into a valley at dawn, or how last light catches a ridgeline. I shoot primarily on medium format and process with a minimal, true-to-scene approach.

Available for editorial, commercial, and fine art print commissions.
